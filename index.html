// HOW I WORK

From Your Idea to a
Live Product — Step by Step.

No guesswork. No surprises. Every project follows the same proven 5-step system — so you always know what's happening, what's next, and when you ship.

01

Discovery

Day 1

We start with a focused call to understand your idea, your users, and your business goal. I ask the right questions to make sure we build the right thing — not just what you asked for.

02

Scope & Plan

Days 2–3

I define the MVP feature set — cutting everything non-essential. You get a clear project scope, timeline, and milestone plan before a single line of code is written.

03

Design & Build

Weeks 1–3

I build your app in Flutter with Firebase — clean architecture, readable code, and a UI that looks and feels premium. You get regular progress updates throughout.

04

Test & Refine

Week 4

Every screen is tested on a real Android device. I fix bugs, polish interactions, and make sure your app is stable and ready before it ever reaches your users.

05

Launch

End of Week 4

I handle the full Play Store submission — listing, screenshots, metadata, and review. Your app goes live. You start getting real user feedback from day one.
